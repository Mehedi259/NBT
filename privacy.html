Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At NeuroBrainTec, accessible from neurobraintec.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by NeuroBrainTec and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

The personal information that you are asked to provide, and the reasons why you are asked to provide it, will be made clear to you at the point we ask you to provide your personal information.

If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website and enterprise applications.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website operations.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our digital platforms.
  • Develop new products, services, features, and AI-driven functionalities.
  • Communicate with you for customer service, to provide you with updates and other information relating to the website, and for marketing and promotional purposes.
  • Send you emails and logs.
  • Find and prevent fraudulent activities and secure our enterprise architecture.

3. Log Files

NeuroBrainTec follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, NeuroBrainTec uses cookies. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

5. Contact Information

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at hello@neurobraintec.com.
